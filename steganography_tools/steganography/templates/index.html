A steganography is the practice of hiding information or a file within a digital photograph, video, or audio file. It will not be obvious to a person observing the object that the information is hidden inside if he or she views it. This will prevent the person from decrypting the information. A steganography can be divided into text steganography, image steganography, audio steganography, and video steganography. Steganography is one of the most common ways to conceal information in cover images. Cover files are embedded with information using the LSB algorithm, which is a very efficient algorithm

Where can you use a Steganography?

Confidential Communication:

Sensitive information can be safely transmitted using steganography over open channels without drawing attention. The covert communication can be maintained by hiding the secret message within seemingly innocent cover media, like pictures, audio files, or video clips.

Copyright Protection

Steganography can be used to add digital signatures or invisible watermarks to media files. This enables content producers to demonstrate ownership and track down illegal use or copyright violations.

Covert Operations:

Steganography can be used for covert communications in military and intelligence operations. To maintain confidentiality, agents can transmit covert messages through a variety of channels or conceal sensitive information within seemingly innocent files.

Digital Forensics:

Steganography can be employed for forensic examination of digital media. In order to collect information about illegal activities or cybercrimes, investigators can find and extract hidden information from suspect files.

Anti-Censorship Tools

Steganography can help people get around internet censorship by hiding sensitive information within seemingly unimportant data. Users can share restricted content or get around content filtering systems using it.

Secret Messaging in Social Media:

Steganography techniques can be used to conceal messages within posts, comments, or images on social media. As a result, people can converse in private on public platforms without raising any suspicions.
